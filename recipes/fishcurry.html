Fish curry

If you're trying to add more fish recipes to your menu, you've got to try this curry! It's a mild but flavor-packed curry in which fish is cooked in a coconut milk broth with aromatics and fresh herbs, and served with aromatic basmati rice. A simple and very satisfying meal! This recipe is inspired by the south-Indian delicacy called Kerala Fish Curry and comes together easily in under 30 minutes, using the Instant pot or stove.
